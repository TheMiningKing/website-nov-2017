Why Mine Cryptocurrencies?

Investment

It's risky, but managing risk is at the heart of investment and entrepreneurship. You might lose all your money, or you might get rich. It's happened before.

Opportunity

Billions have been made in Bitcoin. As with new coins, competitive miners are incentivized to maintain and support various blockchains all around the world.

Transparency

Anyone can examine a blockchain. Though not impossible, it makes it harder to get ripped off when you're making ordinary financial transactions. No trust required.

Anonymity

Transparent and anonymous? Yes indeed. Though every transaction is recorded in the blockchain ledger, there are no names attached. Send and receive coins anonymously.

Highly Secure

A sensible, educated coin-owner is virtually immune to theft. Traditional fiat currencies, and the structures built around them, are easy to defraud by comparison.

World Changing

New coins are solving new problems. Smart blockchain contracts will, in all likelihood, mitigate the need for lawyers, accountants, and government workers.
